The shop advisor agent (currently supports headphones - bounded by data)is a ReAct agent with the ability to call tools. 
Its a simple agent with 3 tools - Product search, Price comparision, safety checker

 Flow
 User query: "Best headphones under $200 for travel"

 Agent thinks:
 Category: headphones
 Constraints: Price < 200
 Use case: Travel


Agent calls the product search tool to identify the matching products.
Product list is then sent to the LLM for further filtering based on price and other attributes.
The product then goes through the safety rules for further filtering
Then the product ids of the shortlisted product sent to the price compare tool to find where to buy the product
